AFRICAN IT PROFESSIONALS

This blog focuses on the struggles or rather the challenges that Africans whom take IT as a profession face.

IT professionals covers a wide range of job description: from HTML/CSS coder to Front-End development and responsibilities.

Skill gap

Skill gap is the difference in skill of IT professionals and that of people seeking jobs in the IT profession. It is very costly to close the gap but this is a crucial step that IT companies should invest in.

Cloud computing

Cloud computing refers to saving and accessing of data on the internet instead of saving it on your hardware. Lot of people looking for jobs in IT are still used to the traditionl way of saving and accessing data.

Cybersecurity

Cyberattackers are growing rapidly in size whilst cybersecurity jobs remain unfilled. This is due to lack of relevant skills from job seekers. into successful cybersecurity professionals.

Digital transformation

Digital transformation is latest disrupter. It has led to technology no longer providing a sustained competitive advantage.
